Biosignal Research Lead

Waterloo, ON



Watolink is a Neuroengineering Design Team focused on developing a brain-computer interface (BCI) model to translate EEG signals into specific user commands. Waterloop

Propulsion Team

SEPTEMBER 2021 - Present

Waterloo, ON



Waterloop is a hyperloop design team formed as a response to the 'SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Challenge'. As a member of the Propulsion Team, I had the opportunity to explore the possibility of electromagnetic levitation within the hyperloop pod, through the utilization of a linear induction motor (LIM). I also had the opportunity to analyze and repair the LIM coil configuration, as well as hand-repair physical components of the motor.
